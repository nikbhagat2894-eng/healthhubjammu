About Health Hub

Health Hub was founded in Gandhinagar, Jammu, with a simple mission: to create a sanctuary where people can reconnect with their bodies and minds. Our studio offers a welcoming space for practitioners of all levels, from first-timers to seasoned yogis.

We believe in holistic wellness — combining asanas, breathwork, and mindfulness. Our certified instructors guide you every step of the way, ensuring a safe and transformative practice.
